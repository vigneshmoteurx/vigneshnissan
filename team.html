CEO

Vignesh

In FY 2020-21, Vignesh Nissan @Puducherry retained its leadership position in the industry as an authorised dealer for Nissan commercial cars, which is the backbone of the domestic business. In comparison to the previous year, the company increased its volume. This is a great showing against the backdrop of a poorer second half of the year, which was impacted by increased axle load regulations, liquidity constraints, and lower demand.
